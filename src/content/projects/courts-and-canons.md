<p>The SHARIAsource project, based at the Program in Islamic Law at Harvard Law School, aims to provide tools to support scholarship relevant to 1400 years of Islamic legal history, including search, visualization and analysis tools. Courts and Canons is a component of the SHARIAsource project, which I worked on as a consultant and deployed as part of my work with PIL.</p>
