A companion digital platform for *Water Stories: River Goddesses, Ancestral Rites, and Climate Crisis* (PI Jinah Kim), an exhibition at Harvard Radcliffe Institute (September–December 2023) exploring the connection between climate change and our experience of water through the lens of communities in the Global South, especially Indigenous ones.

The site documents the physical exhibition and invites visitors to submit their own water stories, with browsable maps and word clouds over the growing community archive. Worked alongside Cara Buzzell (design) and PhD candidates Raghunath Akarsh, Toby Wu, Vaishnavi Patil, and Victoria Andrews.
