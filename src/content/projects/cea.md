<p>The Cambridge Encyclopedia of Anthropology (CEA) is a growing open-access teaching and learning resource. Its goal is to facilitate access to scholarship in Social Anthropology for experts and non-experts worldwide. All entries are written and peer-reviewed by leading academics.</p><p>I have worked as the primary site maintainer / developer / general IT support on the CEA since 2017.</p>
