A Django monorepo for Harvard Law School's Program in Islamic Law that will eventually supercede all of the HLS/PIL project sites — SHARIAsource, Courts & Canons, the Harvard Islamic Law Blog, and the PIL website — behind a single application, search index, and editorial workflow.

Stack: Django + Vue on Postgres with Elasticsearch for search, deployed to AWS via Terraform.
