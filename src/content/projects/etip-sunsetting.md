An HUIT Emerging Technology and Innovation Program (ETIP) initiative addressing the lifecycle management of digital research projects across Harvard. We developed an appraisal framework to evaluate sunsetting options, investigated web-recording platforms for preserving digital scholarship, and prototyped ephemeral environments to lower the operational cost of keeping complex applications available long-term.

Collaborators: Arthur Barrett (Senior Technical Architect, FAS) and Tricia Patterson (Digital Preservation Services, Harvard Library).
