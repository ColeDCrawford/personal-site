A companion site for *Dharma and Puṇya: Buddhist Ritual Art of Nepal* (PI Jinah Kim), an exhibition showcasing a millennium of Newar artisan work from the Kathmandu Valley — paintings, sculptures, illustrated texts, and ritual implements, many on display in the West for the first time.

I built the WordPress site and exhibition catalog alongside Cara Buzzell (design) and Ryan Muther (developer).
