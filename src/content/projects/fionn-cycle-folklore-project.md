<p>The Fionn Cycle Folklore Project has a catalogue of ~4,000 records relating to Fenian folklore resources preserved in archives, and in published sources. These records are classified by type, form, country, institution/collection, and by information about their location, and associated people (collector, source, informant).</p><p>The project (PI: Natasha Sumner) is in active development, and should be released in 2021. I am the primary technical consultant / project manager on the Harvard side, and maintain the project infrastructure.</p>
