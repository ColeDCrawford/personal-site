<p><em>Laboring-Class Poets Online&nbsp;</em>is a substantial component of my MA thesis (2017, Oregon State University). It grew out of the work of John Goodridge, Bridget Keegan, and a team of eighteenth and nineteenth-century British literature scholars who compiled key biographical and bibliographic information on over 2,000 British working-class poets, such as vital dates, identity markers, occupations, interactions with other laboring-class writers and institutions, key publications, and secondary and reference sources.</p><p>From 2013-2015 I structured this data, and as part of my MA thesis I then created <em>LCPO,&nbsp;</em>a Drupal website which provides an accessible way for scholars to access the structured data through maps and other visualizations.</p>
