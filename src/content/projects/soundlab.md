<p>The Sound Lab gives all Harvard affiliates access to cutting edge tools for audio capture and recording, digital media editing, as well as audio mixing, mastering, and restoration. It consists of four Mac-based workstations that run a variety of media production software including Avid&#39;s ProTools and Sibelius, Apple&#39;s Final Cut Pro and Logic Pro, Ableton Live, and Adobe&rsquo;s Creative Cloud.</p><p>The SoundLab website was originally built by Archimedes Digital on Wordpress + React; I maintain the current website and have done substantial extensions to it, including testing and integrating the Mixtape Maker application (Svelte), developed with Haroldson Art &amp; Design in 2020.</p>
